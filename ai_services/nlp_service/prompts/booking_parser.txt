Booking Parser Prompts

Please provide your booking details in the following format:

1. **Booking Type**: Specify whether it's a hotel, flight, or car rental.
2. **Location**: Mention the city or destination.
3. **Check-in Date**: Provide the date in YYYY-MM-DD format.
4. **Check-out Date**: Provide the date in YYYY-MM-DD format (if applicable).
5. **Number of Guests**: Indicate how many people will be staying or traveling.
6. **Preferences**: List any specific preferences (e.g., non-smoking room, window seat).

Example Input:
- "I want to book a hotel in New York from 2023-12-01 to 2023-12-05 for 2 guests, with a preference for a non-smoking room."
